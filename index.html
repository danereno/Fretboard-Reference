Need help learning the layout of the fretboard? Just select how many frets are on your guitar, then select the mode you want to highlight notes in. Hover over a note to see the locations of all similar notes. For example, hovering over F#3 in pitch mode will show you all the places on the fretboard where you can play the exact same pitch.
